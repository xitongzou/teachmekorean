Grammar

Showing Causation

  • V. + (아/어/여)서
  • N. + (이)라(서)
  • V. + (으)니까

Showing future action

  • V. + ㄹ/을 거예요
  • A.V. + (으)려고 하다
  • A.V. + ㄹ/을게(요)

Asking or Suggesting

  • V. + ㄹ/을까(요)?
